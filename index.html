ENTRY CONDITIONS

Every project, employee and business partner is carefully selected by the capability to share our values. We seek this qualities in your future employees and employers.

Energy

To have spark in the eyes,
drive to learn in order
to be the best in given field
and courage to make mistakes
and to learn from them.

Network

To be recognized in professional community, share know how,
set trends and have a rich network of interesting contacts.

Team Player

To be a team player, have the urge to support others, educate them, grow with them and share respect towards different ideas.

Values

To be responsible, trustworthy
and authentic. To have a need to improve the world
and work on meaningful projects.
And breath integrity.

Innovative

To instigate, lead and establish professional community or institute. To link other influencers from different fields to one another.

Personality

To have a great personality and be generous.
To constantly cultivate innner-self and to aim towards common well being.

Tech Skills

To be able to technically manage given crafts, to have the urge
to be the best and do the best job. To have a 100% focus on given tasks.

Positive

To be positive, cheerful, and to able to work but also relax and have fun. To positively manage uncertain situations and to able handle failure.
